About

Operations research
for the real world.

My research sits at the intersection of operations management, manufacturing systems, human-machine collaboration, and economic development.

I am an Assistant Professor of Industrial and Logistics Engineering at Bowling Green State University, where I also serve as Program Coordinator for Systems Engineering. I am the founder and principal investigator of AGORA Lab — the Applied Global Operations Research & Analytics Laboratory.

I examine how technology adoption, particularly AI and digital tools, reshapes operations, labor, and organizational outcomes across a wide range of contexts: from advanced manufacturing in Northwest Ohio to smallholder cooperatives, SMEs, and emerging trade systems across the Global South.

My methodological home is operations research and management science — optimization, MCDM, field experiments, facility layout, and logistics system design. I am committed to research that is applied, global, and honest about the communities it studies.

Before joining BGSU, I was Head of the Industrial Management Department at Liwa College (Emirates College of Technology) in Abu Dhabi. I hold a PhD in Industrial and Logistics Engineering from Ibn Tofail University, Morocco.

Research

Four pillars, one question.

All my research traces back to a single animating question: when intelligent systems enter a workplace or economy, who gains decision-making power — and who loses it? The four pillars below represent the sustained lines of inquiry through which I pursue that question.

A

Applied human–AI decision systems

Studying how workers and managers make better operational decisions when AI is a partner — especially in high-mix, low-volume manufacturing environments where data is scarce.

NSF GOALIHMLV manufacturingIndustry 5.0
G

Global South operations & cooperative management

What does technology adoption look like inside artisanal cooperatives, smallholder operations, and platform sellers in resource-constrained economies? How do advanced methods transfer across scales?

Agency FundJ-PAL MITSchmidt Foundation
O

Operations research & manufacturing systems

Designing and optimizing industrial systems — facility layouts, logistics networks, recycling operations, production planning — under real-world constraints.

NOICFacility layoutMCDMOptimization
R

Regional development & trade management

How digital infrastructure changes physical trade flows, labor markets, and economic agency — and what management frameworks help SMEs capture that value in emerging economies.
